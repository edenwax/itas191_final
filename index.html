About Me

Hey there I'm Colin! A web designing and web developing tech junkie Canadian and ITAS student living it up on the west best coast. When building sites I typically use HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, normalize.css(or sanitize.css), FontAwesome or Google's Material Icons. I'm always trying to learn new and better techniques and eventually want to start getting into webapps using a MEAN stack. That'll be fun, right?

I probably play too many video games, but currently I'm loving Forza Horizon 4, Cities Skylines, Sims4(again), and I'm thinking I'm going to start Red Dead Dedemption 2 again.. *cough* after exams, of course..

Oh, and uh I'm 25 years young, I like long walks on the beach, I enjoy drinking craft beers and copious amounts of coffee, I'm a cat and dog person and my favourite colour is red.
